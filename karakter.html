Batman

Why Batman

  • Strong Moral Code : Batman is my favourite character because of his strong morals. He refuses to kill, even with dangerous criminals, because he can see the good and light in everyone.
  • Noble Intentions : Batman is driven by his deep desire to protect Gotham to ensure no one else suffers the way he did. He is determined to always help others, despite his own trauma.
  • Humanity and Discipline : Batman, without his superpowers, has his strength from his intelligence, discipline, and morals. This shows that doing good things isn't only dependent on whether you have the ability to do it, but also if you are willing to do it.

    • About Batman

  • A superhero from DC Comics
  • Named Bruce Wayne, a wealthy businessman
  • He witnessed his parents' murder, inspiring him to become Batman
  • Operates using his gadgets, intellect, martial art skills, and fear to fight crime
  • Debuted in Detective Comics #27, published in May 1939
